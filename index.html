⚠️ Estimated commission only. Actual amounts may vary based on final contract terms, adders, and adjustments.

  1. Enter your Rep Redline ($/W) in the Base EPC field — this is your cost floor per watt.
  2. Select the number of panels on the system design.
  3. Select the number of Powerwalls included in the deal.
  4. The Redline PPW will calculate automatically — enter this value as the price per watt in Sunraise to generate the proposal.
  5. Once the proposal is built, grab the total contract price from Sunraise and enter it in the Contract Price field to see your estimated commission.
